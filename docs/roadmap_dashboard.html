§4 What the benchmark taught us

Feature preservation

On a cube, QuadriFlow rounds the sharp edges and tears holes (20% slivers); our feature-aware remesh keeps them crisp. A real win even where QuadriFlow leads on raw angle.

The median gap needs a rewrite

Bulk valence-4 quads already match QuadriFlow (80.6°) but are only ~21% of the mesh; ~700 spurious singularities drag the rest. Local edge-rotation cleanup was tried — it trades topology for geometry and nets out negative. Only a global integer parametrization closes it.

Adaptivity now works

A per-vertex spacing (local density) stops the extractor over-merging adaptive input — the count no longer collapses (8× more quads) and adaptive sizing beats our own uniform on 4 / 5 models. Beating QuadriFlow absolutely waits on Phase 4.
